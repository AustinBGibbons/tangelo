This application lets you explore a phylogenetic tree, which expresses evolutionary relationships among species. Click on a node to expand/collapse its descendants recursively.

Modern species appear in the leaves of the tree, while the nodes represent common ancestors of the modern species that lived at various times in the past.

WARNING: hostnames in this configuration are relative to the server (e.g., "localhost" refers to the webserver itself, not your local machine).
